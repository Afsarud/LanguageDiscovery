Rules

Personal Information

It is banned to give out your personal details, such as public email address, home address, phone number etc. Also we advise you to use only your first name and not your surname.

Polite Language

Please be respectful to the other side because you are communicating with a friend in another country and culture. Any rude comments will be reported to us straight away.

Have fun!

Acceptable Use Policy

Students Responsibilities:

Use appropriate and sensible language. At all times be polite in your messages. If the language would be acceptable to send to your family or teachers then it is acceptable to use on your message.

  1. Students will not use obscene, profane, lewd, vulgar, rude, inflammatory, threatening, or disrespectful language.
  2. Students will not post information that could cause damage or a danger of disruption.
  3. Students will not engage in personal attacks, including prejudicial or discriminatory attacks.
  4. Students will not harass another person. Harassment is persistently acting in a manner that distresses or annoys another person. If students are told by a person to stop sending messages, they must stop.
  5. Students will not post personal information about themselves or other people. Personal information includes public email address, home address,phone number, etc.
  6. Students are expected to report any inappropriate use of the Language Discovery Online system to teachers or Language Discovery Online staff.

Penalties:

1st Breach: A warning will be issued to the student and a notification will be sent to the teacher of the student.

2st Breach: The students ID and password will be deleted and the students school notified.

Message

      How do I use Phrase Palette?

This is very simple and easy.
First, decide a category which you wish to talk about from the pull down menu. Then choose phrases and click cells following the indicated numbers.

      How do I use Word Palette?

If you wish to alter a part of the phrase to adjust the contents yourself, Click pen icon left corner  you want to change the word and choose a suitable word from the Word Palette. In case, make sure your cursor is in the right spot.

      Free Message

Input your free message by using a keyboard.

*When you write in Japanese, only Hiragana is acceptable and need space between words.

      Create original word (vocab)

If you wish to alter a part of the phrase to adjust the contents yourself, Click pen icon left corner  you want to change the word and create the word directly in the field by using the add/replace word

      I want to send Message

Click on "Send" at the top-right of the page.
If an alert message shows up, follow the instruction and correct as necessary. Then click on "Send" again.

      I want to use Emoji (sticker)

Click on the  and select Emoji or sticker. Send it to your friend with your message!

       Sending a message to specific friend

Find your friend in Online Friends or Friends box and click one of avatar to jump to the friends room. Click his/her name to send a message.
